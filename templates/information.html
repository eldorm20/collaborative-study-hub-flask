About {{ app_name }}

Welcome to the Collaborative Study Hub! This platform is designed to be a central place for students to share and access learning resources for their courses.

Our aim is to foster a community of learners who support each other by providing notes, summaries, links to helpful materials, and a space to ask and answer questions.

How it Works:

  • Browse Courses: On the homepage, you'll find a list of available courses based on the resources that have been uploaded.
  • Course Dashboard: Clicking on a course will take you to its dedicated dashboard, where you can find a list of shared resources and recent questions.
  • Share Resources: If you have helpful study materials, you can upload them to the relevant course page.
  • Ask Questions: If you're stuck on a topic, feel free to ask your questions in the course's Q&A section.
  • Help Others: If you know the answer to a question, please contribute by sharing your knowledge.
  • User Profiles: (Coming Soon) In the future, you'll be able to see your contributions and manage your profile.

Our Goals:

  • To create a free and accessible repository of study materials.
  • To facilitate peer-to-peer learning and support.
  • To make studying more efficient and collaborative.

Thank you for being a part of the Collaborative Study Hub!
